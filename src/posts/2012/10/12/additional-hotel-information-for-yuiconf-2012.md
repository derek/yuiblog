[YUIConf 2012](http://yuilibrary.com/yuiconf/2012) will be held **Nov 14 - 15** at the [Santa Clara Mariott](http://www.marriott.com/hotels/travel/sjcga-santa-clara-marriott/). We have additional hotel information for you as you set up your travel plans:

**Marriott Santa Clara** (Conference Hotel)

2700 Mission College Boulevard

Santa Clara, CA

Yahoo has secured a [special reduced rate of $189/night](http://www.marriott.com/hotels/travel/sjcga-santa-clara-marriott/?toDate=11/16/12&groupCode=yyuyyua&fromDate=11/13/12&app=resvlink).

Be sure to [check out their FAQ](http://www.marriott.com/hotels/fact-sheet/travel/sjcga-santa-clara-marriott/) for parking, wireless, and other information

**Avatar Hotel**

4200 Great America Parkway

Santa Clara, CA

For the budget conscious, this hotel is across the street from the Marriott. Call (408) 235-8900 and ask for the Yahoo rate.

**Hyatt Regency Santa Clara**

5101 Great America Parkway

Santa Clara, CA

The Hyatt is less than a 5 minute drive from the Marriott. Call (408) 200-1234 and ask for the Yahoo rate.
