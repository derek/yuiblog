[![Yahoo! frontend engineer and YUI contributor Gonzalo Cordero speaks at YUIConf 2010 at Yahoo! HQ in Sunnyvale, CA.](/yuiblog/blog-archive/assets/yuiconf2010-cordero-20101129-150952.jpg)](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/video.php?v=yuiconf2010-cordero)

Yahoo! frontend engineer, [BayJax](http://www.meetup.com/BayJax/ "BayJax - The Bay Area Ajax and JavaScript Meetup (Palo Alto, CA) - Meetup") organizer and YUI contributor Gonzalo Cordero ([@goonieiam](http://twitter.com/#!/goonieiam)) discusses his recently-released [YUI 3 Gallery TreeView](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/yui3treeview "YUI Library :: Gallery :: YUI3-Treeview") implementation. This lighter incarnation of Treeview is built upon YUI 3's Parent-Child module and already boasts a feature-rich and easy-to-implement API and a robust set of events.

If the video embed below doesn't show up correctly in your RSS reader of choice, be sure to [click through to watch the high-resolution version of the video on YUI Theater](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/video.php?v=yuiconf2010-cordero).
