Open Hack Day Taiwan](http://openhacktaiwan.pbworks.com/%E6%B4%BB%E5%8B%95%E8%AD%B0%E7%A8%8B). Among a stellar group of speakers is Thomas Sha, who was the first frontend engineering manager at Yahoo! in 2001 and founded the [YUI project](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/) in 2005. He'll be speaking on Saturday, October 17, at 10:40 a.m. about the recent release of [YUI 3](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/3/). The event is being held at National Taiwan University Hospital (NTUH) International Convention Center.
