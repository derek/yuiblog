[YUIConf 2012](http://yuilibrary.com/yuiconf/2012) is drawing closer and we are renewing the call for speakers. We have already received many excellent proposals and we want to give you more time to send in yours as well!

The new deadline for sending proposals is **Wednesday, October 10**. Submit your proposal to **yui-events (at) yahoo-inc.com**. This is an excellent opportunity to share your knowledge of YUI with the greater community. All topics are welcome! (Share your presentation ideas [here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jAqaUDd0M3NCIuL9Q-iO6zQX9CJVtPvSUY9yLF6zOs0/edit#heading=h.74jk7prp69zj).)

Please include the following information in your proposal:

-   Title
-   Description
-   Intended audience
-   Your name
-   A brief bio (any previous talks? papers?)
