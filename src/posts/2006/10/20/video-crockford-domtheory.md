**Update 20 October 2006:** This video was originally posted with no link to the slide deck that Douglas uses in the talk. That deck is in PowerPoint format and [can be downloaded here](/yuiblog/blog-archive/assets/crockford/theory.zip).

[Douglas Crockford](http://yuilibrary.com/theater/douglas-crockford/) is Yahoo!'s leading JavaScript Architect. He has [written extensively](http://www.crockford.com/javascript/) on JavaScript and has been among the protagonists of the JavaScript developer community for more than a decade. Douglas is the discoverer of the [JSON data format](http://www.json.org) and a [frequent contributor to YUIBlog](http://search.yahoo.com/search?_adv_prop=web&x=op&ei=UTF-8&fr=op&va_vt=any&vp=douglas+crockford&vp_vt=any&vo_vt=any&ve_vt=any&vd=all&vst=on&vs=yuiblog.com&vf=all&vm=p&fl=0&n=30).

At Yahoo!, Douglas has been teaching a series of classes on the JavaScript programming language. These classes bear his unique style and perspective and benefit from the depth of his insights into the language and its application in the browser. We're beginning to make some of this content available internally on video and thought we would share it here as well.
