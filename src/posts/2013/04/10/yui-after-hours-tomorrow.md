Come join us for an evening of informal presentations, conversation, and food!

Join the YUI team, committers, and collaborators for short presentations on what's going on in the YUI community and meet fellow YUI developers. The evening promises to be full of great conversation and plenty of food!

We will be meeting up at Yahoo! HQ in Surf's Cafe on the first floor of Building E at 700 First Avenue, Sunnyvale, CA.

Talks will start at **6pm**, with doors opening at **5pm**.

Please take a moment and [sign up now via Eventbrite](http://yuiafterhours.eventbrite.com/)! This helps us get an idea of how many folks are coming. **The event is free and open to everyone.**

The evening's agenda is listed below.

-   5:00 PM - Doors open. Pizza is served.
-   6:00 PM - Presentations start (15 minutes + 5 minutes in between).
    -   Dav Glass - Opening
    -   Eric Ferraiulo - YUI 3.10pr1
    -   Tilo Mitra - YUI CSS
    -   Ryan Grove - Y.Tree
    -   Luke Smith - Promises
-   Discussions, Mingling, Q&A.
-   8:00 PM - Doors close.

The hashtag for this After Hours event is #yuiafterhours.

We hope to see you there!
