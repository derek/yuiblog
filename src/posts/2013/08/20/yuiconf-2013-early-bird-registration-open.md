We are excited to announce that [early bird registration for YUIConf 2013 is now open](http://www.regonline.com/yuiconf2013)! Our 5th annual conference will be held at the [Hyatt Place](http://sanjose.place.hyatt.com/en/hotel/home.html) in downtown San Jose on **November 6-7**.

Early bird registration is $50.00 until **October 25th**, and $75.00 afterwards.

Conference sessions will be video-taped and available via our [YouTube channel](http://www.youtube.com/channel/UCTHcgWOTU6gPje1g_U29tfQ) for everyone to watch and enjoy.

_(Note: there is no separate registration for Yahoo! employees.)_
