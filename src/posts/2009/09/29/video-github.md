Dav invited the [GitHub](http://github.com/) team down to Yahoo! recently to talk about one of our favorite subjects (Git) and one of our favorite sites (GitHub). Tom Preston-Werner, Chris Wanstrath and Scott Chacon all stopped by and conducted an excellent tag-team tech talk. Video from that session is now up on YUI Theater with a full transcript. Thanks to the GitHub guys for coming down and letting us share this content with everyone on video.
