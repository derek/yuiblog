In this session from [YUIConf 2010](http://yuilibrary.com/yuiconf2010/), Yahoo! engineering intern Tilo Mitra ([@tilomitra](http://twitter.com/tilomitra)) introduces the YUI 3 data-lifecycle toolkit, including facilities for data sources, schemas, and the new-for-3.3.0 YUI DataTable widget.

If the video embed below doesn't show up correctly in your RSS reader of choice, be sure to [click through to watch the high-resolution version of the video on YUI Theater](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/video.php?v=yuiconf2010-mitra).

-   [**Caridy Patiño Mayea:** YUI 3 Loading Strategies: A Yahoo!
