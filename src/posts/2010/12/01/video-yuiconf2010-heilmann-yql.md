In this session from [YUIConf 2010](http://yuilibrary.com/yuiconf2010/), [Christian Heilmann](http://wait-till-i.com/ "Christian Heilmann's blog – Wait till I come!") ([@codepo8](http://twitter.com/codepo8)) describes how to use [YQL](http://developer.yahoo.com/yql/ "Yahoo! Query Language - YDN") sensibly to create robust, innovative applications with immediate scalability.
