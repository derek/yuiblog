The event is free and open to the public; Symantec is hosting the event [on its Mountain View campus](http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&hl=en&geocode=&time=&date=&ttype=&q=350+Ellis+Street+Mountain+View+CA&sll=37.397028,-122.052162&sspn=0.009052,0.016007&ie=UTF8&ll=37.397335,-122.053213&spn=0.008967,0.014677&z=16&iwloc=addr&om=1).
