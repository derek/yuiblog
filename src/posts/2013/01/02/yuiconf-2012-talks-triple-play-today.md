The holidays are over and you've probably returned to the office to find your inbox full of fun things to catch up on, right? ## Ryan Cannon on Using YUI to Tackle Video

In this YUIConf 2012 talk, Ryan Cannon, the manager of application development for the NFL, deep-dives into how the NFL leverages YUI to provide a rich, trackable, and monetizable video experience to its users regardless of device.

## Lightning Talks: Tic Tac Loader, Gear, Mjata Charts and Behavior-Driven Development for YUI

In this series of short lightning talks from YUIConf 2012, Gopal Venkatesan of Yahoo! Mail talks about the Yahoo! Mail add-ons called Tictacs ([slides](http://www.slideshare.net/g13n/tictacs-yahoo-mail-addons)). Stephen Murphy of Yahoo! Finance talks about Mjata Charts ([slides](https://docs.google.com/file/d/0B125RpMEgV12SjdWdlpQcVBsRmc/edit?usp=sharing)). Simon Hojberg from Swipely describes Behavior-Driven Development for YUI ([slides](https://speakerdeck.com/hojberg/bdd-wat)).

## Daniel Stockman on A Poignant Guide to Y.App

At this YUIConf 2012 talk, Daniel Stockman, a front-end engineer at Zillow, talks about refactoring Zillow's most-trafficked pages using YUI App Framework. He reflects on his team's experience with YUI, the structure of their code, and the art of compromise in fast-paced software development.
