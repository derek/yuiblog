Jenny Donnelly is the author of YUI's [DataSource](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/datasource/), [AutoComplete](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/autocomplete/) and [DataTable](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/datatable/) components and the lead editor of [YUIBlog](/blog-archive/). In this session, "Hacking with YUI," Jenny provides an overview of the library and some strategies for leveraging its power in a rapid prototyping environment.

Jenny identifies four key problems that YUI helps to solve:

1.  Bringing data into the page dynamically;
2.  Providing widgets that help build out the UI;
3.  Providing widgets that allow the user to interact with information;
4.  Providing mechanisms for making data user-editable.

[Slides](/yuiblog/blog-archive/assets/hacking-with-yui/hacking-with-yui.ppt) and [code samples](/yuiblog/blog-archive/assets/hacking-with-yui/examples/) from Jenny's talk are available for download.

Our thanks to Eric Wu for organizing the Hack Day sessions and for allowing the YUI Theater crew to shoot Jenny's talk.
