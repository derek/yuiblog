-   Announcing YUI After Hours! Come on over to Yahoo! HQ in Sunnyvale, CA next Thursday evening where we'll be hosting an event featuring short presentations, and conversations about the latest development going on in the world of YUI. Doors open at 5pm, talks begin at 6. Head over to [eventbrite.com](http://yuiafterhours.eventbrite.com/#) for registration and additional details.
    
-   In this week's release news, finishing touches are being put on the first 3.10 preview release. If you are curious what to expect, check out the [dev-3.x compare view](https://github.com/yui/yui3/compare/v3.9.1...dev-3.x). Most notably at this point will be performance improvements in Base ([PR #574](https://github.com/yui/yui3/pull/574)), and you can get a glimpse of those effects on downstream components on [this gist](https://gist.github.com/derek/5321971). YUI 3.10 is [currently scheduled](https://github.com/yui/yui3/wiki/Development-Schedule#wiki-next-release) for release on April 16th.
    
-   This week's Open Roundtable discussion ([YouTube](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=buCNASYrWJM)) featured discussion on [Eventx](https://groups.google.com/forum/?fromgroups=#!topic/yui-contrib/pp3rrHiAUG8), YUI 3.10 PR1, [Y.promise](https://yuilibrary.com/yui/docs/promise/) and the [A+ spec](http://promises-aplus.github.com/promises-spec/), next week's [YUI After Hours](http://yuiafterhours.eventbrite.com/) event, [DOM Futures](http://dom.spec.whatwg.org/#futures), and pull requests.
    
-   Discussion about [memory management](https://groups.google.com/forum/?fromgroups=#!topic/yui-contrib/hP-Qg2jLQXo) continued this week on [yui-contrib](https://groups.google.com/forum/?fromgroups=#!forum/yui-contrib). Thanks to Marco Asbreuk for his [detailed write-up](https://groups.google.com/d/msg/yui-contrib/JUMFhceKl_I/QmBHegJEQRgJ) on the subject, and be sure to check out Eric Frigot's [explanation](https://groups.google.com/d/msg/yui-contrib/hP-Qg2jLQXo/8lnPQf5qv9AJ) between `node.destroy()` and `node.destory(true)`.
    
-   New and updated [Gallery](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/) modules include: [formmgr](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/formmgr), [layout-datatable](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/layout-datatable), [quickedit](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/quickedit), [treeble](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/treeble), and [yqlmock](https://github.com/luchenghan/yqlmock)
    
-   In devtool news, [Shifter](https://github.com/yui/shifter) was updated to **0.3.0** and now defaults --istanbul to true. [Yogi](https://github.com/yui/yogi) was updated to **0.1.0** to include the latest shifter. [yUglify](https://github.com/yui/yuglify) was updated to **0.1.4** to fix colors for non-terminal environments. Upgrade with `npm install -g shifter yogi yuglify`.
