YUI 3 Gallery Contest 2010

Yahoo! and the YUI Team are pleased to announce the YUI 3 Gallery Contest for 2010. We'll be awarding a JSConf 2010 conference pass and a $500 gift certificate to Expedia.com to the prize winner.

To enter the contest, submit a new YUI 3 Gallery module under YUI's BSD license between 12:01 a.m. on March 5, 2010, and 11:59 PST on March 22, 2010. YUI team judges will evaluate entries based on:

  1. Usefulness (50% of total score): to what extent does the module appear to have lasting value to the YUI developer community?
  2. Uniqueness (20% of total score): to what extent is the module a unique contribution, or a contribution with unique characteristics, relative to other YUI 3 Gallery entries?
  3. Quality of Code (15% of total score): to what extent is the module engineered in a way that exemplifies best practices in frontend engineering?
  4. Documentation (15% of total score): are there some good examples? is there enough documentation for an implementer to get started using this module?

The full contest rules are the last word on how the contest is run, so please be sure to read them before you get started. Here are a couple of things to keep in mind:

  • submitter must be a resident of one of the 50 United States or the District of Columbia;
  • submitter must be 18 years of age or older;
  • submitter must not work for Yahoo!;
  • submitter must have submitted a valid YUI Contributor's License agreement by the contest's closing date (CLAs can be downloaded from the YUI Community page);
  • submission must be a new YUI 3 module and must be submitted between 12:01 a.m. on March 5, 2010, and 11:59 PST on March 22, 2010;
  • submission must be offered for use under YUI's BSD license.

Ready to Get Started?

Here are some key resources:

  • YUI Community Page: Download and print the CLA — fax a provisional copy to us and drop the printed copy in the mail. You'll need to have an account on YUILibrary.com and GitHub (both free) to complete your CLA.
  • Read the YUI Gallery Developer Tutorial on YUILibrary.com: This is your best reference for the process of submitting a Gallery module.
  • Watch Dav Glass's presentation on contributing to YUI: This talk from YUICONF 2009 is an excellent launchpad if you're new to the YUI contribution process.
  • YUI 3 Gallery on GitHub: Fork the YUI 3 Gallery project.
  • Check out the YUI 3 Gallery: Reviewing the current set of modules is a good way to get inspired to start creating. You may also find some inspiration from this forum topic in which people have posted some ideas for potential Gallery modules.
  • Questions? The YUI 3 Gallery Forums are at your service.
