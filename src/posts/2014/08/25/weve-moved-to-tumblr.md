As of today, look for YUI-related announcements on the [Yahoo Engineering Tumblr blog](http://yahooeng.tumblr.com/) as well as [YUI’s official Twitter page](https://twitter.com/yuilibrary). The YUI Blog will continue to live in its present form for several more months, albeit in read-only mode. At some point in the near future, it will be archived so that all the great content posted here over the past 8 years remains available to the frontend community.
