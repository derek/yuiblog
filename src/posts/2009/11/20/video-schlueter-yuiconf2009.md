[![YUI engineer Isaac Schlueter at YUICONF 2009, held at the Yahoo! HQ in Sunnyvale; October 28, 2009.](/yuiblog/blog-archive/assets/schlueter-yuiconf2009-yui3-20091110-103941.jpg)](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/video.php?v=schlueter-yuiconf2009-yui3)

Isaac Schlueter ([@izs](http://twitter.com/izs)) is developing the [YUI 3](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/3/) version of [AutoComplete](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/autocomplete/). In this [YUICONF 2009](http://yuilibrary.com/yuiconf2009/) session, "[Solving Problems with YUI 3](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/video.php?v=schlueter-yuiconf2009-yui3)," he shows you how he's working with the core YUI 3 toolkit to address the various challenges inherent in developing a complex widget.

If the video embed below doesn't show up correctly in your RSS reader of choice, be sure to [click through to watch the high-resolution version of the video on YUI Theater](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/video.php?v=schlueter-yuiconf2009-yui3); the downloadable version is much smaller, optimized as it is for iPods, iPhones, and other handheld devices.
