_Welcome to YUI Weekly, the weekly roundup of news and announcements from the YUI team and community. If you have any interesting demos or links you’d like to share, feel free to leave a comment below._

-   This week saw the release of **YUI 3.12.0-beta-1**, the first release candidate for the upcoming YUI 3.12 due out later this month. This release includes various improvements and fixes from 23 contributors across [Button](http://stage.yuilibrary.com/yui/docs/button/), [Charts](http://stage.yuilibrary.com/yui/docs/charts/), [Event](http://stage.yuilibrary.com/yui/docs/event/), [ModelList](http://stage.yuilibrary.com/yui/docs/model-list/), [Node](http://stage.yuilibrary.com/yui/docs/node/), [TabView](http://stage.yuilibrary.com/yui/docs/tabview/), [Template](http://stage.yuilibrary.com/yui/docs/template/), and [Tree](http://stage.yuilibrary.com/yui/docs/tree/). Check out the [release announcement](/yuiblog/blog/2013/08/15/yui-3-12-0-release-candidate-1/) for all the details. The [next milestone for Sprint 9](https://github.com/yui/yui3/wiki/Development-Schedule#sprint-9-next-release) is the Aug 20th [commit freeze](https://github.com/yui/yui3/wiki/Contribution-Standards#commit-freeze) pull request deadline.
    
-   [y3d](https://github.com/brunofarache/y3d/blob/master/README.md) is a [WebGL](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WebGL) module that is new to the [Gallery](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/) this week. Check out the [playground](http://brunofarache.github.io/y3d/) for more demos. Nice work [Bruno](https://github.com/brunofarache)!
    
-   At this week’s [Open Roundtable](https://github.com/yui/yui3/wiki/Open-Roundtable), discussion topics included [3.12-0-beta-1](/yuiblog/blog/2013/08/15/yui-3-12-0-release-candidate-1/), the [new release branch](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!msg/yui-contrib/BFMaCqS6oiE/rdvOZq-2ZVcJ), and a chat with [Rashad Russell](http://github.com/rashadrussell) about his summer as a YUI intern. You can find a video of that Hangout [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Hs_5OrjUoAk).
    
-   Recent threads on the [contributor mailing list](https://groups.google.com/forum/?fromgroups=#!forum/yui-contrib) include new discussion about [default parameters](https://groups.google.com/forum/?fromgroups=#!topic/yui-contrib/yJ8lOK9RHjo) and continuations of earlier threads on ["smart" ModelLists](https://groups.google.com/forum/?fromgroups=#!topic/yui-contrib/68XyipCOqSo) and [Button improvements](https://groups.google.com/forum/?fromgroups=#!topic/yui-contrib/NroWMOr-51g).
    
-   New and updated modules in this week’s [Gallery build](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/buildtag/gallery-2013.08.15-00-45) include: [bulkedit](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/bulkedit), [itsacheckbox](https://github.com/ItsAsbreuk/itsa-gallery/tree/master/src/gallery-itsacheckbox), [itsamodellistsyncpromise](https://github.com/ItsAsbreuk/itsa-gallery/tree/master/src/gallery-itsamodellistsyncpromise), [itsamodelsyncpromise](https://github.com/ItsAsbreuk/itsa-gallery/tree/master/src/gallery-itsamodelsyncpromise), [itsanodepromise](https://github.com/ItsAsbreuk/itsa-gallery/tree/master/src/gallery-itsanodepromise), [itsawidgetrenderpromise](https://github.com/ItsAsbreuk/itsa-gallery/tree/master/src/gallery-itsawidgetrenderpromise), [layout](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/layout), [rocket](http://github.com/camsong/yui.rocket), [y3d-anim](https://github.com/brunofarache/y3d/tree/master/src/y3d-anim), [y3d-camera](https://github.com/brunofarache/y3d/tree/master/src/y3d-camera), [y3d-color](https://github.com/brunofarache/y3d/tree/master/src/y3d-color), [y3d-geometry](https://github.com/brunofarache/y3d/tree/master/src/y3d-geometry), [y3d-light](https://github.com/brunofarache/y3d/tree/master/src/y3d-light), [y3d-math](https://github.com/brunofarache/y3d/tree/master/src/y3d-math), [y3d-picker-plugin](https://github.com/brunofarache/y3d/tree/master/src/y3d-picker-plugin), [y3d-scene](https://github.com/brunofarache/y3d/tree/master/src/y3d-scene), [y3d-shader](https://github.com/brunofarache/y3d/tree/master/src/y3d-shader), and [y3d-texture](https://github.com/brunofarache/y3d/tree/master/src/y3d-texture).
