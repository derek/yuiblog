_Welcome to YUI Weekly, the weekly roundup of news and announcements from the YUI team and community. If you have any interesting demos or links youâ€™d like to share, leave a comment below or email contributions to [weekly@yuilibrary.com](mailto:weekly@yuilibrary.com)._

-   This week saw the official release of [YUI 3.8](/yuiblog/blog/2012/12/12/yui-3-8-0-y-color-templates-and-more/), which introduces [Y.Color](http://yuilibrary.com/yui/docs/color/), new [templating utilties](http://yuilibrary.com/yui/docs/template/), observability in Attribute and Base, [ScrollView enhancements](https://github.com/yui/yui3/blob/v3.8.0/src/scrollview/HISTORY.md#380), and much more. Happy upgrading!
    
-   In "[YUI Moving from Builder to Shifter](/yuiblog/blog/2012/12/13/yui-moving-from-builder-to-shifter/)", Andrew Wooldridge gives an overview of [Shifter](https://github.com/yui/shifter/), YUI's new Node.js-based build tool.
    
-   In this week's YUI Open Roundtable we discussed Promises, [Open Sauce](http://sauceio.com/index.php/2012/12/announcing-open-sauce-free-unlimited-testing-accounts-for-oss-projects/), performance benchmarking, [Ticket #2532677](http://yuilibrary.com/projects/yui3/ticket/2532677), static analysis tools, Widget Popup, and more. You can find video of that meeting [here](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HM-oiG0bGTM), and check out the [YUI Open Roundtable wiki](https://github.com/yui/yui3/wiki/Roundtable-Topics) for more details on previous and future Roundtables. We also created an [Open Roundtable](http://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjKP9DUCzZoqAKuA4P4llDb6BR_obpeV_&feature=plcp) playlist on our YouTube channel.
    
-   New [YUIConf 2012](http://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjKP9DUCzZooMmiSJ_nmsLIReOZp0Uomz) videos this week: Douglas Crockford's keynote "[Monads and Gonads](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dkZFtimgAcM)" and Evan Goer's "[Writing for Developers](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3ZOmXKl4OhQ)"
    
-   Daniel Barreiro (aka: Satyam) [contributed a post](/yuiblog/blog/2012/12/14/the-flyweight-pattern/) to describe "The Flyweight Pattern" and introduce the [FlyweightTreeManager](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/flyweight-tree) and [FWTreeView](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/fwt-treeview) Gallery modules.
    
-   A user on Quora asked "[How does YUI 3 achieve responsive web design?](https://www.quora.com/YUI/How-does-YUI-3-achieves-responsive-web-design-RWD)"
    
-   In "[YUI From the Inside](http://www.wonko.com/post/yui-from-the-inside)", YUI contributor Ryan Grove ([@yaypie](https://twitter.com/yaypie/)) praises the project for introducing more transparency and ease of contribution, which he requested a few months ago in "[YUI from the Outside](http://www.wonko.com/post/yui-from-the-outside)". Thanks Ryan!
    
-   Luke, Tilo, and Eric [got together](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v7Z6FlO1opU&feature=plcp) talk about issues around the `tap` event and the native DOM events it's built on.
    
-   Version bumps this week for the following YUI devtools: [grover](https://github.com/davglass/grover), [shifter](https://github.com/yui/shifter), [yogi](https://github.com/yui/yogi). Upgrade with `npm install -g shifter grover yogi`.
    
-   New and Updated modules in the [Gallery](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/) this week: [alea](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/alea), [array-unnest](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/array-unnest), [bottle](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/bottle), [button-plugin](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/button-plugin), [composite-image](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/composite-image), [datatable-paginator](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/datatable-paginator), [datatable-selection](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/datatable-selection), [flyweight-tree](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/flyweight-tree), [fwt-treeview](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/fwt-treeview), [paginator-view](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/paginator-view), and [weighted-list](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/weighted-list).
