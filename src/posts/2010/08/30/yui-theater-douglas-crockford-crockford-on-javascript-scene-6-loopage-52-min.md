Douglas Crockford's latest installment in the "[Crockford on JavaScript](/yuiblog/crockford/)" series, a talk in which he covers the role of event loops and the importance of server-side JavaScript, is now available on video. Flash video is embedded below, or you can [download the HD video (480p ~370MB)](http://bit.ly/duCFAh). ### Other Recent YUI Theater Videos:

-   [**Nicholas Zakas and Victor Tsaran:** Accessibility on the Yahoo Homepage](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/video.php?v=zakas-tsaran) — Nicholas Zakas, a principal developer of the Yahoo! homepage, and Victor Tsaran's, Yahoo!'s senior accessibility manager, discuss the strategies and methods that made one of the most visited websites in the world fully accessible.
