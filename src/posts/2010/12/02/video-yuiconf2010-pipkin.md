[![YUI contributor Anthony Pipkin of The Jones Group speaks at YUIConf 2010 at Yahoo! HQ in Sunnyvale, CA.](/yuiblog/blog-archive/assets/yuiconf2010-pipkin-20101129-144300.jpg)](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/video.php?v=yuiconf2010-pipkin)

Anthony Pipkin ([@apipkin](http://twitter.com/#!/apipkin), Interactive Director at The Jones Group), a prolific YUI contributor, presents the first part of a two-part series on how to move from implementing simple functionality in YUI 3 to producing modular, reusable components that leverage YUI 3's component infrastructure (including widgets and plugins). ([Part 2, from fellow YUI contributor Pat Tivac, is also available on YUI Theater](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/video.php?v=yuiconf2010-cavit).)

If the video embed below doesn't show up correctly in your RSS reader of choice, be sure to [click through to watch the high-resolution version of the video on YUI Theater](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/video.php?v=yuiconf2010-pipkin).
