[![Yahoo! internationalization architect Norbert Lindenberg speaks at YUIConf 2010 at Yahoo! HQ in Sunnyvale, CA.](/yuiblog/blog-archive/assets/yuiconf2010-lindenberg-blog.jpg)](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/video.php?v=yuiconf2010-lindenberg)

On the web as in the real world, every language is a minority language. To reach a majority of users, your software will need to support multiple languages and be adapted to the needs and expectations of different cultures. In this session from [YUIConf 2010](http://yuilibrary.com/yuiconf2010/), Yahoo! internationalization architect Norbert Lindenberg discusses how to do this while building on YUI 3. (Note: Due to a microphone malfunction, there is a short gap in the video and the portion of the video after the gap uses ambient audio.)
