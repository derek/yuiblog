_Welcome to YUI Weekly, the weekly roundup of news and announcements from the YUI team and community. If you have any interesting demos or links you’d like to share, feel free to leave a comment below._

-   This week saw the release of **Pure v0.2.0**, an update that includes improvements to [Forms](http://purecss.io/forms/), [Grids](http://purecss.io/grids/), and [Menus](http://purecss.io/menus/). In addition, there were accessibility tweaks and the introduction of `grunt test`. You can find all the details on this release at [purecss.io/updates/](http://purecss.io/updates/), the [changelog](https://github.com/yui/pure/blob/master/HISTORY.md#020-2013-06-11), and the [v0.1.0...v0.2.0 diff](https://github.com/yui/pure/compare/v0.1.0...v0.2.0). Thanks to the **8 contributors** for their **90 commits** that make up **v0.2.0**!
    
-   In other Pure news, [generator-pure](https://github.com/sindresorhus/generator-pure) is a [Yeoman generator](http://yeoman.io/generators.html) for [Yo](https://github.com/yeoman/yo). So if you are a Yeoman/Yo user, including Pure in your application is now as simple as `$ npm install -g generator-pure` then `$ yo pure`.
    
-   At this week’s [Open Roundtable](https://github.com/yui/yui3/wiki/Open-Roundtable) ([video](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JtY-qHQ0Q9g), [notes](https://gist.github.com/derek/5777819)), discussion topics included the [recent YUI releases](/yuiblog/category/releases/), [Pure 0.2.0](http://purecss.io/updates/), a [Datatable](http://yuilibrary.com/yui/docs/datatable/) update and [roadmap](https://trello.com/board/datatable-roadmap/518a5e5af277b61271001c3c) review, a Responsive grids demo, and of the usual [Up for Grabs](https://github.com/yui/yui3/issues?direction=desc&labels=up+for+grabs&sort=created&state=open) and [Stale pull requests](https://github.com/yui/yui3/pulls?direction=asc&page=1&sort=updated&state=open) review.
    
-   7 new [pull requests](https://github.com/yui/yui3/pulls) this week, including [some](https://github.com/yui/yui3/pull/871) [updates](https://github.com/yui/yui3/pull/873) to [Y.Tree](https://yuilibrary.com/yui/docs/tree/), a [cleanup of /build/](https://github.com/yui/yui3/pull/865), a [DOMReady bug fix](https://github.com/yui/yui3/pull/853), and more. Thanks contributors!
    
-   [Shifter](https://github.com/yui/shifter/) recieved a version bump this week from [v0.4.0 to v0.4.1](https://github.com/yui/shifter/compare/v0.4.0...v0.4.1) this week to [add support](https://github.com/yui/shifter/pull/86) for assets on CSS modules.
    
-   New and updated modules in this week’s [Gallery build](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/buildtag/gallery-2013.06.13-01-19) include: [itsadialog](https://github.com/ItsAsbreuk/yui3-gallery/tree/master/src/gallery-itsadialog), [itsadialogbox](https://github.com/ItsAsbreuk/yui3-gallery/tree/master/src/gallery-itsadialogbox), [itsaerrorreporter](https://github.com/ItsAsbreuk/yui3-gallery/tree/master/src/gallery-itsaerrorreporter), [itsamodulesloadedpromise](https://github.com/ItsAsbreuk/yui3-gallery/tree/master/src/gallery-itsamodulesloadedpromise), [itsaselectlist](https://github.com/ItsAsbreuk/yui3-gallery/tree/master/src/gallery-itsaselectlist), [itsaviewmodelpanel](https://github.com/ItsAsbreuk/yui3-gallery/tree/master/src/gallery-itsaviewmodelpanel), [itsawidgetrenderpromise](https://github.com/ItsAsbreuk/yui3-gallery/tree/master/src/gallery-itsawidgetrenderpromise), [layout](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/layout), [layout-cols](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/layout-cols), [layout-rows](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/layout-rows), and [scrollintoview](http://yuilibrary.com/gallery/show/scrollintoview).
