[BayJax](http://www.meetup.com/BayJax/ "BayJax - The Bay Area Ajax and JavaScript Meetup (Palo Alto, CA) - Meetup.com") returns to Yahoo! on December 11 with a talk from [jQuery](http://jquery.com/) creator [John Resig](http://ejohn.org/ "John Resig - JavaScript Programmer") ([@jeresig](http://twitter.com/jeresig "Follow John on Twitter.")).

> ### JavaScript Testing and Performance Analysis
> 
> **[Yahoo! HQ in Sunnyvale. Building E, Classrooms 9 & 10.](http://bit.ly/5yJkGz) Pizza at 5:30 p.m., talks start at 6:15 p.m.**
> 
> A two-part talk: A comprehensive look at what you need to know to properly test your JavaScript code and an analysis of the popular JavaScript performance testing techniques and their shortcomings.
> 
> **[Reserve a seat](http://www.meetup.com/BayJax/calendar/11949987/).**

[BayJax](http://www.meetup.com/BayJax/ "BayJax - The Bay Area Ajax and JavaScript Meetup (Palo Alto, CA) - Meetup.com") events at Yahoo! are free to the public, and we'd love to see you there. [Head over to the BayJax site to register](http://www.meetup.com/BayJax/calendar/11949987/ "Save a seat for the December BayJax event at Yahoo!").
