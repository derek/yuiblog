In this session from [YUIConf 2010](http://yuilibrary.com/yuiconf2010/), YQL engineer Nagesh Susarla explores the power of YQL execute in the creation of powerful YQL open data tables. (If you're new to YQL, don't miss Myrek Grymuza and Josh Gordineer's "[Introduction to YQL](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/video.php?v=yuiconf2010-introtoyql "Video: Mirek Grymuza and Josh Gordineer — YQL: An Introduction (YUI Theater)")," also from YUIConf and available on [YUI Theater](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/ "YUI Theater").)
