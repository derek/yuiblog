[Yahoo! Sports](http://sports.yahoo.com) has reached 36 million unique visitors per month and dominates the online Sports and Fantasy Sports industry worldwide. The site’s recipe for success: high quality content presented via rich and standard-compliant interfaces.

The Y! Sports experience is so engaging, users view over 2 billion pages per month — more than Fox, SI, and CBS Sports combined! To meet such high demands, Y! Sports stays at the forefront of Internet technologies by leveraging platforms such as [YUI](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/).

If you consider yourself an avid [YUI](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/) user, then you’re on your way to joining their team.

Email your resume, including portfolio URLs, to mediatech-hiring /at/ yahoo /dash/ inc /dot/ com (Principals only; no recruiters, please.) or [find out more about the position](http://careers.yahoo.com/jdescription.php?frm=jsres&oid=25874).
