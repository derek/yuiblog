Update: This internship has now been filled!

The YUI team is looking for a summer intern who has completed at least one year of formal coursework toward a CS degree and who wants to gain first-hand experience writing industrial-strength JavaScript code. This is your opportunity to work alongside the YUI team, contributing to all levels of the project. Both undergrads and graduate students are welcome to apply.

This internship will be a good fit for you if:

-   you love JavaScript;
-   you're familiar with best practices of frontend engineering;
-   you're not afraid of big challenges;
-   you're a full-time student at an accredited college or university.

Email a cover letter, resume, and link to your portfolio to [yui-jobs@yahoo-inc.com](yui-jobs@yahoo-inc.com). Be sure to mention your dates of summer availability.
