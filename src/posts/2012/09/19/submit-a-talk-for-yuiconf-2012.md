You've worked hard on your YUI-based project all year and now is the time for you to share what you've learned! Submit your proposal to **yui-events (at) yahoo-inc.com** by Wednesday, October 10. Please include the following information in your proposal:

-   Title
-   Description
-   Intended audience
-   Your name
-   A brief bio (any previous talks? papers?)

YUIConf will be held on November 5-6 (Mon-Tue) at the Santa Clara Marriott. Your presentation should last around 45 minutes with 15 minutes more for Q and A. Post any questions in the comments below, or email us directly at **yui-events (at) yahoo-inc.com**. Please give as much information as you can in your description. If you know of anyone who has done something significant with YUI this year, please encourage them to consider presenting. It's a great opportunity to share what you've learned with other members of the YUI community.
