We're looking for an exceptional JavaScript engineer to join us in building the next generation of open-source JavaScript platforms. Our products solve real-world, complex problems at massive scale, have flexible and intuitive APIs that have withstood the test of time, and embrace the best practices of usability, testability, DRY, and performance. This position is located in Sunnyvale, CA, and telecommuting is not available at this time.

Required Job Qualifications:

-   Computer Science or equivalent degree.
-   Absolute mastery of JavaScript.
-   Expertise in OO and agile software development with a proven track record of problem solving and quality.
-   A passion for the YUI family of products, including Mojito, and the problems they are striving to solve.
-   Excellent communication and collaboration skills, great attention to detail and follow-through.

Preferred Job Qualifications:

-   Experience delivering platforms for other developers.
-   A commit history on open source software projects, especially YUI or Mojito.
-   Depth of expertise in one or more front-end domains such as:
    -   cross-browser UI
    -   mobile constraints
    -   performance
    -   JavaScript on the server
    -   front-end testing frameworks
    -   internationalization
    -   the MVC problem space

To apply, please send your resume and cover email to yui-jobs (at) yahoo-inc.com. Be sure to include pointers to your GitHub account and/or code samples.
