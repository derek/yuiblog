[John Resig](http://ejohn.org), an engineer at Mozilla and the creator of [jQuery](http://jquery.com/), stopped by Yahoo last week to visit with the YUI team. While here, he gave a talk for Yahoo engineers on the current state of JavaScript and DOM programming. The session -- entitled "The DOM Is a Mess" -- harvests many of John's insights from his work on jQuery and related projects, and it offers guidance about how to think about some of the most challenging aspects of in-browser software development.

Our thanks to John for the visit and for allowing us to share his talk with you here on [YUI Theater](http://developer.yahoo.com/yui/theater/).
